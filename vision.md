# Executive Summary

NextStack is an AI-powered career navigation platform designed to help technology students make informed learning decisions based on their existing skills, career goals, and evolving industry trends.

The modern technology landscape changes rapidly, causing students to struggle with questions such as:

* What should I learn next?
* Which technology stack is most relevant today?
* Is a particular skill becoming saturated?
* How do my skills compare to market demand?
* Which learning path best aligns with my career goals?

NextStack aims to become the personalized career operating system for students by combining skill tracking, roadmap generation, market intelligence, portfolio management, and AI-driven guidance into a unified platform.

---

# Problem Statement

Students often follow generic roadmaps, social media trends, or random recommendations when deciding what to learn.

As a result:

* Learning paths become inefficient.
* Students spend months acquiring low-impact skills.
* Career decisions are based on assumptions rather than data.
* Skill development lacks direction and measurable progress.

Current platforms provide learning content, coding practice, or portfolio hosting, but none provide personalized recommendations that combine current skills, career objectives, and market demand.

---

# Vision

To become the most trusted platform that guides students from learning to employment by helping them identify the right skills at the right time.

---

# Mission

Enable every student to answer one critical question:

"What should I learn next?"

using data-driven recommendations instead of guesswork.
